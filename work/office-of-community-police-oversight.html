Office of Community Police Oversight

UI Design

‍Role: UI Designer
Programs: Adobe XD, Illustrator & Photoshop
Year: 2025

The Office of Community Police Oversight is a department established to ensure complaints against Dallas Police Department (DPD) officers are investigated thoroughly and transparently.

Challenges: We received an extensive outline of updates the stakeholders wanted and required. We had to review all content and develop unique component that aligned with the City's web standards. We had to ensure the business requirements were met while also advocating user needs and accessibility concerns. We also under went a change in leadership that added new level of standard development in our design process and component refined to meet the satisfaction of the new management that came in mid project.

Project Outcome: Months of effort, working collaboratively and iteratively we were successful in meeting all requirements set from business and user standpoints. This project helped to refine many components within our style guide which will be reused for other department sites.
