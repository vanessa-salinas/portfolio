Planning and Urban Design

UI Design

Worked closely with the PUD department stakeholders, product manager, UX designers and front end developers to create visuals based on sketches and requirements from the department.

Role: UI Designer
Programs: Adobe XD, Illustrator & Photoshop
Year: 2020

Challenges: Working with a department who had their own ideas and mockups of what they wanted their website to look like but with no website standards used. Tasked with bringing these ideas under the City of Dallas style guide standards. Including fonts, colors, components and compliant with web accessibility.

Project Outcome: Through close collaboration and an extensive iterative process the department was very satisfied with the final product and expressed that it met their overall purpose of a creative, dynamic and focused online presence to expand the reach of the department’s message, publications, support efforts in educating, informing and encouraging involvement in planning for the city’s future.
