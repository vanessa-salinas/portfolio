Concepts for Dallas City Council Districts

UX/UI Design

There are 14 Council Districts in the City of Dallas. Each district has it's own webpage to showcase content to their constituents. The goal is to redesign and standardize all district pages.
‍
Role: UX/UI Designer
Programs: Adobe XD, Illustrator & Photoshop
Year: 2024-2025

Challenges: Every district has it's own content and set of ideas they present on their webpages. Content inventory was used to create a set of reusable components for each district. Gathering requirements and feedback was key to narrowing and perfecting each section to house all 14 districts needs.

Project Outcome: The district websites are currently under development and in the iteration process.
