Housing and Neighborhood Revitalization

UI Design

Worked closely with the Housing and Neighborhood Revitalization stakeholders, product manager and front end developer to design a new website that includes a new pillar structure to the department.

Role: UX/UI Designer
Programs: Adobe XD, Illustrator & Photoshop
Year: 2023

Challenges: Combining business needs with user needs to create an accessible, responsive, easy to understand website while maintaining the City of Dallas style guide standards.

Project Outcome: The final website has been easier to navigate for internal and external users. Below is a comment from someone who works in the department after launch.

"Amazing!! When I am on the phones I always go to the website, today I clicked on it and thought this is really nice and liked how easy it was to navigate to the different programs. Great Job!"
