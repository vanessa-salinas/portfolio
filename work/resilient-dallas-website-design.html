Resilient Dallas

UX/UI Design

Worked closely with the Resilient Dallas stakeholders, product manager and front end developer to turn an 80 page strategy document into a user friendly website.

Role: UX/UI Designer
Programs: Adobe XD, Illustrator & Photoshop
Year: 2019-2020

Challenges: Taking an 80 page document that was created by a third party and converting it into easy to understand and accessible web pages under the City of Dallas style guide standards.

Project Outcome: The final web pages have served as a place of accountability by providing a live status of each goal set by this strategic document.
